N.Y.S.L    News

The N Y S L soccer team will have a new leader as they defend their 2017-18 Chicago Association state championship next season. The Cook county Union High School District Governing Board approved the hiring of Mike Smith to take over as the boys head coach. Smith, has been involved as a soccer coach since 2010, when he was a volunteer assistant at NorthWestern College. He spent two years as the head coach of Greenbay High School, for the past three seasons.
